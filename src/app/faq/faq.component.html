Frequently Asked Questions
Who can join?
Anyone who wants to learn how to make their own websites, is welcome. Students from all fields, majors, and experience levels can join.
I already know basics. Why should I join?
Good news. We not only have a bootcamp for beginners, but also a module for more experienced programmers, with tutorials on technologies that they want to learn about, along with many tech talks by our corporate sponsors and senior students.
What if I miss a meeting? How do I catch up?
For the beginner track, meeting slides, code, and other content is located at github.com/xhpwn/WDC-Angular2. We also have a Google Drive folder here. Additionally, you can always message or contact one of the instructors or the board to get additional help on learning.
Sounds fun! When does the club meet?
Club meetings are at 6:30 PM. The beginner track usually meets on Mondays and Wednesdays in Physics 331, and the advanced track meets on Tuesdays and Thursdays in Physics 338. These are subject to change, so we recommend keeping an eye on our Facebook page, and signing up for our mailing list here.
